Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients: 2½ cups flour, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tsp baking soda, ½ tsp salt, 1½ cups sugar, 1 cup oil, 2 eggs, 1 cup buttermilk, 2 tsp vanilla, 1 tsp vinegar, red food coloring.

Steps: Mix flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. In another bowl, mix sugar, oil, eggs, buttermilk, vanilla, and red color. Combine both bowls, then stir in vinegar. Pour into pans. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes. Cool and frost with cream cheese frosting.

Vanilla Sponge Cake

Ingredients: 2½ cups flour, 2½ tsp baking powder, ½ tsp salt, 1 cup butter, 1¾ cups sugar, 4 eggs, 1 tbsp vanilla, 1 cup milk.

Steps: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Cream butter and sugar, add eggs one at a time, then mix in flour, baking powder, and salt. Add milk and vanilla. Pour into greased pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes.

Lemon Drizzle Cake

Ingredients: 1 cup of softened unsalted butter, 1 cup of sugar, 4 eggs, 2 cups of self-raising flour, the zest of 2 lemons, and the juice of 1 lemon.

Steps: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Cream butter and sugar, add eggs one at a time, then mix in flour, lemon zest, and juice. Pour into a greased loaf pan. Bake for 50-60 minutes. Drizzle with lemon glaze.

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients: 1 cup of unsalted butter (softened), 1 cup of brown sugar, 2 eggs, 1 ½ cups of rolled oats, 1 cup of flour, ½ tsp baking soda, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1 cup of raisins, and a pinch of salt.

Steps: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, then mix in oats, flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in raisins. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients: 1 cup of peanut butter, 1 cup of sugar, 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla extract, and ½ tsp baking soda.

Steps: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix peanut butter, sugar, egg, vanilla, and baking soda. Roll into balls and flatten with a fork. Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Snickerdoodles

Ingredients 1 cup of butter (softened), 1 ½ cups of sugar, 2 eggs, 2 ¾ cups of flour, 2 tsp cream of tartar, 1 tsp baking soda, and a pinch of salt. For the coating, mix 3 tbsp sugar and 2 tsp cinnamon.

Steps: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, then mix in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Roll into balls, coat in cinnamon-sugar, and place on a baking sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
